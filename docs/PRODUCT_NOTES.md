## Product rules

- Do not show fake progress, fake percentages, fake reports, or fake activity.
- Keep reports at zero until tasks are actually completed.
- Do not show limit-related UI until the family decides whether limits are needed.
- Use child-friendly wording.
- Prefer checklists for children and lightweight status/overview for parents.
- Keep the weekly plan as the source of truth.
